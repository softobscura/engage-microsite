Engage's Capabilities

Cross channel responses are funneled into one central landing page via a single URL, allowing you to easily integrate both online and offline marketing.

Engage generates personalised URL's (PURLs) which can be added to any direct marketing materials.

PURLs direct customers to your existing database to custom landing pages which are pre-populated with their information.

These can all be monitored from the powerful analitics platform that allows you to track and monitor all campaign performance in real time via our Uproduce Dashboard.

By increasing your market intelligence and getting to know customer preferences, you can improve your target marketing for future campaigns.
